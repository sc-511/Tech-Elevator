Metal Gear Solid

"War...War never changes"

About

A series of action adventure stealth video games created by Hideo Kojima and developed and published by Konami. The player often takes control of a special forces operative Solid Snake or Big Boss who is assigned the task of finding the superweapon "Metal Gear" a bipedal walking tank with the ability to launch nuclear weapons.
